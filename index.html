Golden Roasted Milk

Simple and easy to get start, charred outside and tender inside. A feast of taste and sight!
Material
Servings

5

Prep time

120m

Cook time

30m

Detailed steps:


Step 1

Add two egg yolks, 25 grams of sugar, 50 grams of cornstarch, and 500ml of whole milk to a non-stick pan in order.


Step 2

Stir over low heat and add a slice of cheese.


Step 3

Boil slowly until smooth. Transfer to a 6-inch round mold or similar bowl. Chill for at least 2 hours


Step 4

Cut it into 8 pieces after chill


Step 5

Brush the top with egg yolk and bake in the oven at 180 degrees for 25 minutes. Watch out that the surface of the milk does not burn. Done!
